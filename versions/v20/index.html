What v20 adds over v19

  • Verification-source line. Under the live timer: via wi-fi · OfficeWiFi / via location / active tracking.
  • Tier-aware topbar chip. While punched in, the chip label flips from "live" to the actual tier — wifi · geo · gps. Coral dot stays.
  • Shift-end-in countdown. Appears as a 4th cell in the numrow: 18:24 ends. Replaces "Out" placeholder until you actually punch out.
  • Alert line. Hairline mono text that appears only when something is off — low battery, queued uploads, GPS unavailable. Hidden by default.

The tier mapping

  • wifi (T1). Device on approved office Wi-Fi (BSSID match). No GPS pings. Cheapest.
  • geo (T2). Off Wi-Fi but inside office geofence (100m). No GPS pings.
  • gps (T3). Off Wi-Fi, outside geofence. Continuous tracking, 10-min pings.

Colour budget (unchanged)

  • Coral #DC4C3E: live dot, "now" tick, punch CTA fill, scan line. Nothing else.
  • Alert line: still monochrome. Just text-2 mono. No yellow, no red. The text itself is the signal.
